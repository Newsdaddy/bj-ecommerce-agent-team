## Final Content

Coupang now owns 40% of South Korea's eCommerce market.

But that's not the real story.

75% of all online transactions happen on mobile.

South Korea isn't just big—it's the most mobile-first eCommerce market in the world.

Here's what decision-makers need to know:

→ Market size: $160B+ in 2025
→ Online share of total retail: 40%
→ Mobile dominance: 75% of transactions
→ Coupang + Naver combined: 65% market control

The duopoly is real.

But here's where it gets interesting:

**3 trends reshaping the market:**

1. Live commerce is mainstream
   → Not emerging. Mainstream.
   → Brands are selling directly through live streams on Naver and Kakao.

2. Social discovery drives purchase
   → 70% of Koreans find new products through social media.
   → If you're not visible on Korean social, you don't exist.

3. Membership is the moat
   → Coupang's Rocket WOW has 14M+ subscribers.
   → That's 2 out of 3 Korean households locked in.

The playbook is clear:

Speed. Mobile. Membership. Social.

If you're planning APAC expansion, Korea isn't optional—it's the blueprint.

What's your Korea strategy?
