# English Style Guide: Justin Welsh Style

## Justin Welsh Style Characteristics

| Characteristic | Description | Example |
|----------------|-------------|---------|
| **Hook First** | Grab attention in first line | "Here's what no one tells you about..." |
| **Short Lines** | One thought per line | Use line breaks liberally |
| **Numbered Lists** | Structure with numbers | "3 lessons I learned:" |
| **Personal Touch** | Insert personal experience/perspective | "I've seen this happen..." |
| **Action-Oriented** | Call to action | "Here's what to do:" |
| **White Space** | Use spacing | Empty lines between paragraphs |

## Application Example

### Before (Generic Style)
> Vietnam's e-commerce market has experienced significant growth over the past few years, reaching a total value of $14.7 billion in 2024, which represents a 25% year-over-year increase compared to the previous year.

### After (Justin Welsh Style)
> Vietnam's e-commerce market just hit $14.7B.
>
> That's 25% growth YoY.
>
> Here's what this means for your business:
>
> 1. Southeast Asia is no longer "emerging"—it's arrived
> 2. Shopee and Lazada own 80% of the market
> 3. The window for early entry is closing
>
> If you're not paying attention to APAC, you're leaving money on the table.

## Sentence Structure Pattern

```
[Hook - Number or surprising fact]

[One-line interpretation]

Here's what this means:

1. [Insight #1]
2. [Insight #2]
3. [Insight #3]

[CTA or question]
```

Here's why this matters:

   → [One-liner]

   → [One-liner]

```

```

## Expressions to Avoid

| Avoid | Replace With |
|-------|-------------|
| It is important to note that | (Delete) |
| In order to | To |
| A lot of | (Specific number) |
| Very, really | (Replace with data) |
| Due to the fact that | Because |
| In terms of | For / About |

## Writing Checklist

### Sentence Level
- [ ] Max 15 words per sentence
- [ ] Active voice preferred
- [ ] Remove unnecessary modifiers
- [ ] Include specific numbers
- [ ] Strong verbs

### Structure Level
- [ ] Hook in first sentence
- [ ] Numbered lists
- [ ] Bullet points where needed
- [ ] Plenty of white space

### Tone Level
- [ ] Concise and action-oriented
- [ ] Minimal emotional language
- [ ] Fact-focused
- [ ] Conversational but professional

## Regional Considerations

| Region | Style Notes |
|--------|-------------|
| **Australia/NZ** | Direct, informal, no-nonsense |
| **Singapore** | Professional, formal titles matter |
| **India** | Relationship-focused, slightly formal |
| **Southeast Asia** | Warm, relationship-building first |
