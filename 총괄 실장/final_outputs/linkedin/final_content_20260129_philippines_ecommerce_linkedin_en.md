## Final Content

The Philippines just became Asia's fastest-growing eCommerce market.

24.1% growth rate.

Not Vietnam. Not Indonesia. The Philippines.

Here's what most APAC strategists are missing:

→ Market size: $17.65B in 2025
→ Mobile commerce: 79% of all sales happen on smartphones
→ Internet penetration: 83.8% (97.5M users)
→ Digital payments: 52.8% of retail transactions

But the real story is the duopoly battle:

**Shopee vs Lazada**

Shopee: 70M+ monthly visitors. Growing 33% in 1H24.
Lazada: 37M monthly visitors. Declining 10%+ in 1H24.

Shopee is winning. Hard.

And now TikTok Shop is the #2 downloaded shopping app, beating Lazada.

**3 trends reshaping the market:**

1. Mobile-first is an understatement
   → 73.7% smartphone penetration
   → $11.1B m-commerce segment
   → Average 67.6% annual m-commerce growth since 2022

2. Digital wallets are the new normal
   → GCash: 94M users (in a country of 115M)
   → Mobile wallet adoption: 65%+
   → BNPL growing at 15.89% CAGR

3. Young, digitally-savvy population
   → Median age: 25.7 years
   → 170M mobile connections (146% of population)
   → Social commerce is exploding

The Philippines is projected to hit $33.65B by 2030.

That's nearly 2x growth in 5 years.

If you're building an APAC strategy and not looking at the Philippines, you're leaving money on the table.

What's your Philippines play?
